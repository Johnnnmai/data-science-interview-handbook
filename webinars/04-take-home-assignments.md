# Webinar 4: DS Take-Home Analysis Projects

- Date: March 9, 2026
- Host: [Alexey Grigorev](https://www.linkedin.com/in/agrigorev/)

## Description

Take-home analysis projects are where your ability to do end-to-end data science work is truly measured. This session focuses on moving beyond toy datasets to mastering real-world analytical assignments used by top-tier companies.

## Topics Covered

1. Assignment analysis - examining recent Q4 2025 and Q1 2026 submissions to understand current DS hiring standards
2. Implementation discussion - deconstructing real-world assignment prompts and analyzing effective analytical approaches with trade-offs
3. End-to-end data analysis - building a complete solution covering data exploration, statistical testing, modeling, and business recommendations

Practice resource: [The 0→1 Data Scientist Interview Playbook](https://valenx.org/books/ds) includes a full chapter on take-home project execution with real examples, evaluation rubrics, and common pitfalls that cost candidates offers.
